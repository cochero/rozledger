Questions & answers

RozLedger, answered

Is RozLedger free to use?

Yes. The Free plan creates GST invoice text, GST totals, UPI payment links and QR codes, and WhatsApp reminders, and saves up to 5 invoices per month — no credit card required.

How do I make a GST invoice for free?

Enter your business, client, service and amount, choose a GST rate (5%, 12%, 18% or 28%), and RozLedger instantly builds invoice text with the GST total, due date, a UPI payment link and a WhatsApp reminder to copy or save.

What accounting features are included?

A chart of accounts, double-entry vouchers, customer receipts and AR, vendor bills and AP, partial payments, credit and debit notes, FIFO inventory, an audit log, and reports such as profit & loss, trial balance and AR/AP aging.

Who is RozLedger for?

Indian freelancers, tutors and coaching centres, agencies, consultants, traders and local service businesses that bill clients and want simple, reliable daily records.

How are corrections handled?

Posted records are not silently deleted. RozLedger uses credit notes, vendor debit notes, payment reversals and audit logs so your business record stays explainable.

Is RozLedger accounting or tax advice?

No. RozLedger organises business records and workflows. GST filing, tax and legal treatment should be confirmed with a qualified professional.
