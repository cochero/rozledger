Accounts and finance

Built toward complete small-business finance management

What accounting functions are available now?

Customers can save invoices, manage clients, use a chart of accounts and post balanced manual journal entries. Dashboard totals show income and expenses from posted entries.

What is coming next?

The next finance phase should connect payment received, vendor bills, expenses, AP, AR, receipts and reports to the journal system automatically.

Is RozLedger accounting advice?

No. RozLedger helps organize business records and workflows. GST, tax filing and legal treatment should be confirmed with a qualified professional.
