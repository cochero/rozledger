RozLedger policy

Privacy Policy

RozLedger is built to help Indian small businesses prepare billing text, GST calculations, UPI links and payment reminders. This page explains what information may be collected.

Information you enter

The calculator can be used directly in your browser. If you choose to save invoice details or request early access, the information you submit, such as name, phone number, business type and invoice fields, may be stored so we can provide the service, respond to your request and improve the product.

How information is used

Submitted information may be used to operate RozLedger, respond to early-access requests, understand which tools are useful and prevent misuse. We do not claim that RozLedger is an official GST, accounting or legal compliance product.

Third-party services

RozLedger may use hosting, analytics, email, payment or support tools in the future. Any partner or sponsored link should be clearly labelled before it is added.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page and include the phone number or email you submitted so the request can be checked.
