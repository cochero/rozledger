Questions & answers

RozLedger, answered

Is RozLedger free to use?

Yes. The Free plan creates professional invoice text, payment links and QR codes, and reminder messages, and saves up to 5 invoices per month — no credit card required.

How do I make a free invoice?

Enter your business, client, service and amount, add a sales-tax rate if needed, and RozLedger builds invoice text with the total, due date, a payment link and a reminder message to copy or save.

What accounting features are included?

A chart of accounts, double-entry vouchers, customer receipts and AR, vendor bills and AP, partial payments, credit and debit notes, FIFO inventory, an audit log, and reports such as profit & loss, trial balance and AR/AP aging.

Who is RozLedger for?

US handymen, cleaners, lawn care, mobile repair, tutors, consultants, photographers, coaches and other independent service businesses that bill clients and want simple records.

How does accounts receivable work?

Customer payments can be posted against selected invoices, including partial payments, and customer ledger statements show invoices, receipts and balances.

Is RozLedger tax or accounting advice?

No. RozLedger is an invoice and recordkeeping tool. Tax and accounting decisions should be verified with a qualified professional.
