# RozLedger: 15-Day Revenue Plan

## Honest target

`$100/day` is about `₹8,300/day`, or roughly `₹2.5 lakh/month`. A brand-new organic website cannot reliably reach that from ads in 15 days. The fastest realistic route is a useful daily tool plus direct outreach and high-intent affiliate or lead payouts.

## Best business idea

Build a daily small-business utility for India:

- GST invoice text generator
- UPI payment link and QR generator
- WhatsApp payment reminder generator
- Daily revenue target calculator
- SEO pages for invoice formats, GST calculators, rent receipts, payment reminders and local business templates

This works because users can return daily. The content also attracts high-intent searchers: freelancers, shop owners, tutors, agencies, consultants and home-service providers.

## Monetization

Start with these revenue channels:

- Affiliate leads: business credit cards, payment gateways, accounting tools, current accounts, GST filing and business loans.
- Sponsored placements: local CAs, GST consultants and payment service providers.
- Pro plan: `₹299/month` for saved clients, branded invoice PDF, recurring reminders and export history.
- Done-for-you setup: `₹999-₹2,999` one-time service for local businesses who want invoice templates and payment workflows.

## Required math

Approximate paths to `₹8,300/day`:

- 4 qualified finance leads/day at `₹2,000` average payout.
- 28 pro customers/day at `₹299` first-month revenue.
- 9 setup orders/day at `₹999`.
- A blended path: 2 finance leads, 8 pro customers and 2 setup orders.

The blended path is more realistic than relying only on traffic.

## 15-day sprint

1. Day 1: publish the MVP on a fast host, connect analytics and Google Search Console.
2. Day 2: join affiliate networks and replace placeholder links.
3. Days 3-5: publish 30 long-tail pages targeting India-specific use cases.
4. Days 6-8: create 20 short videos: "make GST invoice in 60 seconds", "send UPI reminder", "freelancer payment follow-up".
5. Days 9-11: share helpful templates in freelancer, city, coaching, CA and MSME communities.
6. Days 12-15: contact 50 local businesses per day by WhatsApp and phone. Sell setup service and convert repeat users into pro users.

## Automation roadmap

The static MVP works immediately, but daily automation needs a backend:

- Save invoices and client history.
- Send scheduled WhatsApp reminders through an approved WhatsApp Business provider.
- Generate printable PDFs.
- Create programmatic SEO pages from templates.
- Track affiliate clicks and conversions.
- Send daily email or WhatsApp digest to users.
