RozLedger for Tutors and Coaching Centres

Tutors often collect recurring fees from students or parents. RozLedger helps prepare receipt text, payment reminders and due-date messages quickly, which keeps communication consistent during busy teaching days.

Why this matters

Fee reminders can become uncomfortable when they are typed differently each time. A clear receipt and polite reminder format keeps parent communication professional.

How RozLedger helps

  • Fee receipt text can be prepared quickly after payment.
  • WhatsApp reminders can be copied without sounding harsh.
  • UPI links and QR codes make fee collection easier.
  • Saved invoice records help review what was sent earlier.

Best fit

Best for tuition teachers, home tutors, coaching centres and small training providers collecting monthly or course-wise fees.

Important note

RozLedger does not manage student attendance or academic records. It focuses on fee receipt text, payment links and reminders.
